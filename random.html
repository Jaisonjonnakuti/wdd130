Delicious Home Cooking Recipes

Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. Brown beef in a pan
  2. Add onions and garlic
  3. Pour in tomato sauce
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Cook pasta, serve sauce over top

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken breast
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Stir fry chicken
  3. Add vegetables
  4. Season with sauce
  5. Cook until chicken is done

Vegetarian Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Mix all ingredients
  4. Add dressing
  5. Chill before serving

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Mix wet ingredients
  2. Add dry ingredients
  3. Fold in chocolate chips
  4. Form into balls
  5. Bake at 350°F

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • Arborio rice
  • Mixed mushrooms
  • Vegetable broth
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onions
  • White wine

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions
  2. Add rice
  3. Pour in wine
  4. Add broth gradually
  5. Stir in mushrooms
